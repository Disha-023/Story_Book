Gryffindor

The house of the lion. Helmed by Godric Gryffindor, this group of Hogwarts students tend to be braver than most, and able to hold their nerve in difficult situations.

Slytherin

The house of the snake! Sss! As founded by Salazar Slytherin, this house doesn't have the best reputation. But without Slytherin, you wouldn't have the likes of the great wizard Merlin - or the adorable Scorpius Malfoy. Beyond cunning and determination, this house has hidden depths.

Hufflepuff

The house of the badger! Hufflepuffs are known for being kind and hard-working, as was their house-founder, Helga Hufflepuff, known for her warmth of character and bountiful beasts. We could all do with more Hufflepuffs in our life.

Ravenclaw

The house of the eagle! Rowena Ravenclaw championed those of wit and learning, and Ravenclaw is exactly that. The likes of Cho Chang, Luna Lovegood and Ignatia Wildsmith, the inventor of Floo Powder, hail from this house - and you even need to solve a riddle to get into the Ravenclaw common room. Wit beyond measure truly is man's greatest treasure if you get sorted here.
