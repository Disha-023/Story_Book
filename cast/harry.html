Harry Potter: The Name That Echoes Through Time

"The Boy Who Lived" wasn’t just a title — it was a reminder that courage can grow from the darkest of places. Harry Potter stood not because he was fearless, but because he chose to fight even when he was afraid. He showed us that kindness, loyalty, and love can defeat the darkest magic. From the cupboard under the stairs to saving the wizarding world — his journey inspired millions. He wasn’t born a hero — he became one.
