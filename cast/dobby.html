Dobby – The Free Elf with a Fierce Heart

Dobby was more than a house-elf — he was a symbol of freedom and loyalty. With a pure heart and unshakable courage, he risked everything to protect Harry and his friends. Though small in size, Dobby's bravery outshined the darkest forces of magic. His sacrifice remains one of the most touching acts of love in the wizarding world.
